Mini-Biography


Marie Korbel (JP: マリー・コーベル Marī Kōberu), also known as Bloody Marie (JP: ブラッディー・マリー Buraddī Marī), is the central antagonist of Skullgirls and the current Skullgirl. She appears in every character's Story Mode and in the ninth round of Arcade mode. The boss form of Marie is only playable in Challenge Mode, and at the start of her story mode. She is included as the last Season 1 DLC Pass' regular playable character.
