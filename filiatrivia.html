Mini-Biography


Filia Medici (JP: フィリア・メディチ Firia Medichi) was once a somewhat normal school girl, until her parents were killed by Vitale and Ottomo, with Samson fusing with her to save her life. She is currently homeless and suffering from amnesia. She is the daughter of Amelia and Marcus, making her Lorenzo's granddaughter and Vitale's niece.
