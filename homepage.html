The storyline of Skullgirls is based in the fictional Canopy Kingdom, a country that is heavily reminiscent of the 1940s post-World War II United States, but with a unique blend of high technology, magic, and art deco aesthetics.

The Early Timeline


The game's world is populated by diverse species and groups, including the ruling Renoir royal family, the dangerous Medici Mafia, and the scientific Anti-Skullgirl Labs, all vying for control or dealing with the consequences of the mysterious Skull Heart artifact. The lore and timeline are detailed across the games' story modes, the Skullgirls mobile game, and supplementary materials like webcomics and developer notes.

The playable characters in Skullgirls include the original eight: Filia, Cerebella, Peacock, Parasoul, Ms. Fortune, Painwheel, Valentine, and Double. Additional characters have been added as DLC or through other releases, such as Squigly, Fukua, Beowulf, Big Band, Eliza, Robo-Fortune, Annie, Black Dahlia, Umbrella, and Marie.

These are just a few of the characters on the roster.
