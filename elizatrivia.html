Mini-Biography


Eliza (JP: イライザ Iraiza) is the celebrity diva of New Meridian's premiere nightclub, Bastet's Den. She is the mistress of Albus and Horace, and has been host to the skeletal Parasite Sekhmet for an untold amount of years.
